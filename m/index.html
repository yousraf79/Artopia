41st graphic design exhibition

One of my favorite days in winter 2022:

I visited the 41st graphic exhibition with my uni VCD friends, we saw so many amazing designs. We were so impressed, there were many good examples of:

  • Packaging designs

    This is a packaging design for a patisserie, I felt happy seeing the designer who crafted such visually stunning packaging and presentation for Bonnenuit, a patisserie that I frequented for buying desserts for my friends' birthdays, getting the appreciation they deserved.

  • Stickers

    The stickers at the exhibition were awesome! I especially loved the motivational quote on the plastic bag, the colors, and the font. They were so fine.

  • Posters

    Those posters were amazing! If you're looking for some inspiration, you should definitely go check out the exhibition. They had a wide variety of posters, including ad posters, typography posters, and so much more.

  • Cards

    Although the cards were meant for multiple businesses or companies, I found all of them to be visually appealing and aesthetically pleasing. They were quite eye-catching and grabbed my attention.

I had an amazing time at the exhibition and was inspired by numerous designs. I am excited to share my first blog post with you, and there is so much more that I can't wait to share. Please stay tuned.
